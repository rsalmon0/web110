Human Bean 3

The finale of the Human Bean Saga.

H.M. "Hammer" Hammarin has fallen on hard times in the years following the destruction of his criminal empire and his escape from Miami-Dade federal prison. Hammer's new plan to bring himself back on top will send him (as well as his newfound allies and enemies) on an insane international adventure...


This is my longest in development and most ambitious project I've ever tackled. I've been working off and on on this since 2017. The goal of the project was to create a total conversion mod (most if not all game assets are custom made) that is feature length - clocking in between 6-8 hours across 16 levels. The mod will be free to those who own Hotline Miami 2 and can be found on the Steam Workshop when it is released.






Hammer with the minigun by Jerry Kaczynski

Many members of the community have lent a hand during the mods lengthy development, or help playtest levels to refine the mod's balance. The main poster seen above was made by the amazingly talented Gem Ryan. Jerry Kaczynski has contributed many many sprites and animations and composer S4Breaks has provided two songs for the soundtrack.

The entire mod is created within the Hotline Miami 2 level editor. It is a tool made by the developers to allow levels and cutscenes to be created and strung together by players. Over the years, the community has found/built 3rd party tools to exploit an otherwise extremely limited editor and bend it to our will.
